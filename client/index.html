  • General FAQ
    • Do I need a Dash Waiter account to place an order?
      You must have an account and be logged into your account to place an order. You will automatically be logged out of your account at the close of the event.
    • What type of payment does Dash Waiter accept?
      We accept all major credit cards. You may store up to five different credit cards in your account.
    • Can I pay my Dash Waiter tip in cash?
      Your credit card will be charge a minimum tip of 10%. You will also have the option to tip 15%, or 20% on your credit card. Additional tip amounts can be paid to your waiter in cash.
    • Will orders from separate vendors be delivered at different times?
      Each vendor’s items will be delivered separately and may not all arrive at the same time.
    • If I receive deliveries from more than one vendor, will I receive multiple charges on my credit card?
      If your order is very large or if you order from more than one vendor your order will be split and may be delivered by more than one waiter. However, you will see only one charge on your card per order.
    • How many vendors can I order from?
      You can order from as many vendors as located in the vicinity of your section. Keep in mind that each vendor’s items will be delivered separately.
    • Is there a limit to the number of alcoholic beverages I can order?
      You may order a maximum of two alcoholic beverages per ID stored.
    • Can I cancel an order?
      You will have an opportunity to check and modify your order before it is submitted. Orders cannot be cancelled after they are submitted for processing.
    • Where can I learn more about Dash Waiter?
      Go to www.dashwaiter.com to learn more.
